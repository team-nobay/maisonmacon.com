About

Built Piece by Piece

Maison Maçon was founded on the belief that the most beautiful interiors are not decorated — they are collected. Each piece in our inventory has been sourced personally from estates, flea markets, and private sellers across France, Italy, and Spain.

We specialise in Napoleon III seating, gilt mirrors, marble consoles, patinated bronze, and antique oil paintings — objects that carry the warmth of centuries and the unmistakable character of European craftsmanship.

Isabel Frasier
Founder & Curator
